Let's explore the off-court stories and career milestones of NBA stars together. Using a vast archive of biographies ranging from legendary players to current talent, I've brought together the most inspiring life stories of the basketball world in one platform.

NBA: Legendary Stories is a static informational website, developed entirely with HTML and CSS, designed for basketball fans who want to go beyond statistics. On the site, I've compiled the personal milestones, key moments, and lesser-known stories of five of the game's most iconic players (Kobe Bryant, Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Stephen Curry, and Kevin Durant).

This project consists of a homepage (nba.html) and five separate biography pages (kobe.html, mj.html, lbj.html, curry.html, kd.html), each supported by a fully animated SVG basketball court background working behind the content. (Each HTML file links to its own external stylesheet.)

This repository is currently a simple but visually engaging multi-page informational website project. No backend, framework, or build tool is required.

Animated court background: Created entirely using SVG shapes and CSS @keyframes. I placed a player on the court dribbling, driving to the basket, jumping, shooting, and watching the ball go through the net.

Player bio pages: Each player has their own custom HTML page with bulleted information about their personal journey rather than statistics — childhood, draft story, career-defining moments. For example, Kobe Bryant's page covers his childhood in Italy, his high school legend status, skipping college entirely, and being traded to the Lakers on draft night. (He's my favorite NBA player!)

Fonts: I used Google Fonts' Advent Pro, Geist Pixel, Indie Flower, and Quicksand fonts to give the site a bold, sporty, and editorial feel.

Dark Theme: I kept the background completely black across the entire site so the court animation and orange hoop/ball highlights stand out against the background.

Buttons and Navigation: Each player button on the homepage is individually positioned and styled with rounded corners and a hovering state that changes, transforming into a simple, clickable player list for navigating between stories.

Project Structure

nba.html (Main homepage — animated basketball court background and navigation buttons leading to each player page)
kobe.html (Kobe Bryant biography page)
mj.html (Michael Jordan biography page)
lbj.html (LeBron James biography page)
curry.html (Stephen Curry biography page)
kd.html (Kevin Durant biography page)
